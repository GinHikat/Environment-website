About the Organization

The Organization was founded in 2023 to commence protecting the Environment from potential threats

This includes the following insights:

  • Reduce, reuse, and recycle. Cut down on what you throw away.
  • Volunteer. Volunteer for cleanups in your community.
  • Self-Educate.
  • Conserve water.
  • Choose sustainable.
  • Shop wisely.
  • Use long-lasting light bulbs.
  • Save electricity and fresh water
  • Use resources wisely
  • Educate other people
  • Plant a tree.






Along with that, we also want to promote our own policies and activities:

  • Clean Air Act (CAA)
  • Clean Water Act (CWA)
  • Comprehensive Environmental Response, Compensation, and Liability Act (CERCLA)
  • Endangered Species Act (ESA)
  • Marine Protection, Research and Sanctuaries Act (MPRSA)
  • National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA)
  • Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA)
